# **App Name**: ExamMentor AI

## Core Features:

- Personalized Exam Setup: Allows users to select their target exam and configure a tailored study profile, ensuring content relevance and intelligent adaptation from the start.
- Adaptive Practice Module: Delivers subject-specific practice questions, intelligently adjusting difficulty and topic focus based on user performance.
- AI-Powered Mentor Insights Tool: Provides natural language interpretations of performance data, transforming raw numbers into actionable, human-readable advice and identifying areas of strength and weakness, much like an experienced mentor would.
- Dynamic 'Next Best Action' System Tool: Constantly evaluates a user's current progress and insights to suggest the most impactful next study task or resource, prominently displayed and driving proactive learning.
- Proactive Alert & Indicator Layer: Monitors for patterns indicating potential burnout, fluctuating focus, retention decay, or confidence mismatch, providing gentle, intelligent nudges rather than jarring notifications.
- Progress Narratives & Causal Analysis: Presents learning progress as an evolving story, connecting study efforts to observable improvements or challenges, fulfilling the 'What, Why, What's next' philosophy.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary Color: A serene, desaturated blue (#3973AC) to evoke trust, clarity, and intellectual focus, serving as a versatile anchor for interactive elements and key information.
- Background Color: A very light, almost imperceptible cool-grey with a hint of blue (#F0F2F5), providing a calm, uncluttered canvas reminiscent of premium note-taking applications.
- Accent Color: A clear, vibrant cyan (#52E0E0), carefully chosen for its analogous relationship to the primary hue, ensuring good contrast and bringing an energetic but calm highlight to calls to action or novel information.
- Semantic Highlight Colors: Utilize a restrained palette of intelligent highlights where 'Red' signifies urgency or critical areas, 'Amber' indicates caution or attention needed, and 'Green' confirms stability or successful progress, used sparingly and contextually for mentor-like feedback.
- Body and Headline Font: 'Inter', a highly legible and versatile sans-serif typeface known for its modern, clean aesthetics, providing a neutral and objective foundation suitable for extensive textual insights and optimal readability across all content.
- Spacious and Minimalist Grid: Embrace generous whitespace and an 'Apple-like' approach to spacing, creating a clean, organized, and tranquil visual environment that emphasizes content clarity and reduces cognitive load, akin to Notion's calm interfaces.
- Subtle Line-Art Icons: Implement a collection of minimal, consistent line-art icons that complement the clean aesthetic, focusing on symbolic representation and immediate recognition without visual distraction.
- Purposeful Micro-interactions: Employ subtle and non-intrusive animations, such as gentle transitions or a soft pulse for the 'Next Best Action' button, to provide elegant feedback and guide user attention without overwhelming the user.
